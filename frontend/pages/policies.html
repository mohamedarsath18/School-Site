📜 School Policies

📌 Rules & Regulations

  • 🎓 Students must wear the proper uniform at all times.
  • 📅 Attendance is mandatory for at least 90% of school days.
  • 🙌 Respect towards teachers, staff, and classmates is required.
  • 📵 Mobile phones are strictly prohibited in class.
  • 📚 All students must complete their homework and assignments on time.

🎯 Code of Conduct

  • 🚸 Students must maintain discipline and proper behavior inside the school premises.
  • 💬 Any form of bullying, harassment, or misconduct will result in strict action.
  • 🚯 Cleanliness and hygiene must be maintained at all times.

⏳ Attendance Policy

  • 🏫 Students are expected to be present in school from 8:00 AM - 3:30 PM.
  • 🛑 Late arrivals beyond 15 minutes require prior approval.
  • 📜 Leave applications must be submitted for any planned absences.

🛡️ Health & Safety Policies

  • 🚑 In case of emergency, the school has a medical team on standby.
  • 😷 Students with contagious illnesses should stay home until full recovery.
  • 🏃 Fire drills are conducted regularly for safety preparedness.
